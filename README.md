AgroGuard AI – Smart Crop Disease Detection

AgroGuard AI is an intelligent web-based platform that assists farmers and agriculture enthusiasts in identifying plant diseases through AI-powered image analysis. By uploading a photo of a crop or leaf, users can receive a quick diagnosis along with useful treatment and prevention recommendations.

The application integrates Google Gemini AI to analyze plant images and generate clear, structured reports that help users make better decisions about crop health and management.

Project Overview

Crop diseases and pest infestations are among the major reasons for reduced agricultural productivity. Many farmers face difficulty identifying these problems at an early stage because of limited access to expert advice.

AgroGuard AI aims to address this challenge by providing a digital solution that analyzes plant images and delivers instant insights about possible diseases. The platform is designed to be simple, fast, and accessible so users can easily understand the condition of their crops and take appropriate action.

Core Features

Image-Based Disease Detection
Users can upload a photo of a plant leaf to detect potential diseases.

Live Camera Scanning
The system allows users to capture images directly from their device camera for real-time diagnosis.

AI-Powered Analysis
Google Gemini Vision AI processes the uploaded image and identifies possible plant diseases.

Detailed Diagnosis Reports
The platform provides structured results including symptoms, causes, treatment options, and prevention methods.

Agriculture Knowledge Hub
An integrated encyclopedia provides useful information about crops, diseases, and agricultural practices.

AI Farming Assistant
A built-in chatbot helps answer farming-related questions and provides guidance.

Multi-Language Accessibility
The platform supports multiple languages to improve usability for diverse users.

Responsive and Clean Interface
A modern user interface ensures smooth performance across different devices.

How the System Works

The user uploads or captures an image of a plant or leaf.

The application processes and optimizes the image for analysis.

Google Gemini AI evaluates the image and detects potential plant diseases.

The system generates a structured report containing:

Disease name

Severity level

Symptoms

Causes

Treatment suggestions

Prevention strategies

The results are displayed in a clear and easy-to-understand format.

Project Objective

The primary objective of AgroGuard AI is to support farmers by enabling early detection of plant diseases. By combining AI-based image analysis with practical agricultural knowledge, the system helps reduce crop damage and improve overall farming productivity.
